# Tracking checklist

## Extract from supplier email

- supplier;
- Shopify order number or customer reference;
- carrier;
- tracking number;
- shipped products;
- partial shipment/backorder notes;
- dispatch date if provided.

## Shopify update preparation

Prepare:

- tracking carrier;
- tracking number;
- fulfilled line items if partial shipment details are available;
- note text;
- notification summary.

## Stop conditions

Ask for Daniel review when:

- tracking cannot be matched to one Shopify order;
- carrier is unknown;
- tracking number is malformed or unclear;
- shipped products do not match confirmed products;
- partial shipment details are unclear.
