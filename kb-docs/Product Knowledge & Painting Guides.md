# Product Knowledge & Painting Guides

## Paint Types
- Interior latex/acrylic - for walls, ceilings, trim indoors
- Exterior acrylic - weather-resistant for outdoor surfaces
- Enamel - hard-wearing for doors, trim, cabinets
- Primer/sealer - for surface preparation before topcoat
- Epoxy - for floors, garages, industrial use

## Airless Sprayer Selection Guide
- Small projects (home DIY): Graco Magnum X5/X7
- Medium projects (contractors): Graco 390/395/490
- Large projects (commercial): Graco 595/695/795/1095
- Tip sizes: 311 for trim, 415 for walls, 517 for broad surfaces, 521 for texture

## Coverage Calculations
- Standard paint: approximately 12-16 sqm per litre
- One coat coverage varies by paint type and surface
- Rough/textured surfaces need 20-30% more paint
- Always recommend extra for touch-ups

## Surface Preparation
- Sand glossy surfaces before repainting
- Clean surfaces to remove dust, grease, mildew
- Fill holes and cracks, let dry, then sand smooth
- Apply primer on bare wood, metal, or stained surfaces

## Sanding Equipment
- Mirka DEROS - random orbital sander for fine finishing
- Mirka DEOS - rectangular orbital for flat surfaces
- Mirka Dust Extractors - pair with sanders for dust-free sanding
- Use 80-grit for stripping, 120-grit for prep, 240-grit for between coats

## Masking Tips
- Use quality masking tape for clean paint lines
- Remove tape while paint is still slightly wet
- Use masking paper/film for large protection areas
- 3M and Scotch Blue are recommended brands
