# Product Knowledge & Painting Guides

## Paint Types
- Interior latex/acrylic - for walls, ceilings, trim indoors
- Exterior acrylic - weather-resistant for outdoor surfaces
- Enamel - hard-wearing for doors, trim, cabinets
- Primer/sealer - for surface preparation before topcoat
- Epoxy - for floors, garages, industrial use

## Airless Sprayer Selection Guide
- Small projects (home DIY): Graco Magnum X5/X7
- Medium projects (contractors): Graco 390/395/490
- Large projects (commercial): Graco 595/695/795/1095
- Tip sizes: 311 for trim, 415 for walls, 517 for broad surfaces, 521 for texture

## Coverage Calculations
- Standard paint: approximately 12-16 sqm per litre
- One coat coverage varies by paint type and surface
- Rough/textured surfaces need 20-30% more paint
- Always recommend extra for touch-ups

## Surface Preparation
- Sand glossy surfaces before repainting
- Clean surfaces to remove dust, grease, mildew
- Fill holes and cracks, let dry, then sand smooth
- Apply primer on bare wood, metal, or stained surfaces

## Sanding Equipment
- Mirka DEROS - random orbital sander for fine finishing
- Mirka DEOS - rectangular orbital for flat surfaces
- Mirka Dust Extractors - pair with sanders for dust-free sanding
- Use 80-grit for stripping, 120-grit for prep, 240-grit for between coats

## Mirka Sanders, Abrasives and Dust Extractors

### Mirka Sanders

#### Mirka DEROS 175
The Mirka DEROS 175 uses a 400W motor. Because the 125mm, 150mm and 175mm DEROS models use the same 400W motor platform, the main limitation is not power but compatibility.

The DEROS 175 cannot simply be used with 125mm or 150mm backing pads/discs if the thread or fitting is different. The 175mm disc is not compatible with the smaller models where the thread or mounting system does not match.

#### Mirka LEROS and LEROS-S
The Mirka LEROS and LEROS-S both use a 350W brushless motor.

The key difference is size and handling. The LEROS-S is the shorter version and has a handle near the base, while the full-size LEROS does not have that same handle setup.

No LED light update is currently expected for the LEROS range.

#### Mirka DEROS RS
The DEROS RS is a rotary sander, not a random orbital sander. It is a pure rotary tool.

It is suitable for sanding, buffing and polishing. It has strong torque and is considered very powerful for these applications.

#### Other Mirka Sanders
Mirka also offers Delta and square sanders. These are more straightforward tools used for detailed sanding and surface preparation.

#### Motor Generations
Mirka sanders have gone through two motor generations.

First generation:
- Used a 350W brushless motor.
- The LEROS and LEROS-S still use this first-generation 350W brushless motor platform.
- Despite being the older motor generation, these tools remain highly reliable and continue to be part of the current Mirka range.

Second generation:
- Newer DEROS models have been upgraded to a 400W brushless motor.
- The increase in power provides improved performance while maintaining the low vibration and dust extraction benefits that Mirka is known for.
- The current DEROS 125mm, 150mm and 175mm models use this newer 400W motor platform.

Important note:
- Although the current DEROS range uses a 400W motor, earlier DEROS models were also equipped with the older 350W motor. This is why some specifications found online may reference either 350W or 400W depending on the generation being discussed.
- The LEROS and LEROS-S have not yet received this motor update and continue to operate using the proven 350W brushless motor platform.

### Dust Classes
For painters and construction users, M-class dust extractors are the main focus.

Dust classes:
- L-class: Low hazard dust, such as household dust and general sanding dust.
- M-class: Medium hazard dust, including silica, paint dust, plastering compounds and similar construction dust.
- H-class: High hazard dust, such as asbestos and mould.

M-class vacuums filter 99.9% of dust passing through the vacuum and are suitable for most painting and plastering applications.

Mirka does not currently offer an H-class version in this range.

### Mirka Abrasives

#### Abranet
Abranet is Mirka's base-level abrasive range. It uses aluminium oxide grain.

It provides good dust extraction because of its mesh structure, but aluminium oxide abrasives generally wear faster than ceramic abrasives.

Standard aluminium oxide abrasives may wear out after around 20-30 minutes of use, depending on the application.

#### Abranet Ace
Abranet Ace is a higher-performance abrasive range using ceramic grain.

Ceramic grain is self-sharpening. As it wears, the grain chips and creates new sharp edges instead of simply becoming dull. This helps the abrasive last longer and maintain performance.

Abranet Ace generally has excellent dust extraction and is one of the best options for sanding performance.

Typical grit range: approximately 80 grit to 1000 grit, depending on disc size.

For larger sizes, such as 225mm discs for LEROS, the grit range is smaller because the use case does not require as many grit options.

#### Galaxy
Galaxy is another popular Mirka abrasive range. It is generally a bit cheaper than Abranet Ace.

Galaxy is a film-backed abrasive, making it tougher and more durable. It uses a hole pattern rather than a mesh structure.

Galaxy also uses ceramic self-sharpening grain, so it maintains cutting performance for longer.

Typical grit range: 40 grit to 2000 grit.

## Masking Tips
- Use quality masking tape for clean paint lines
- Remove tape while paint is still slightly wet
- Use masking paper/film for large protection areas
